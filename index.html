📚 Quick Reference Guide

Everything you need to get the most out of your planner. Type to search.

🔍
▶ 👋 Getting Started
✍
Personalise your planner name — On first launch, you'll see a welcome screen where you can name your planner (e.g. "Sarah's Family Meals"). You can change it anytime in Settings > Planner Name.
💰
Set your currency first — Go to Settings > Region & Currency and pick your currency. This affects budget tracking, shopping links, and health profile units (imperial for US/CA/MX, metric for others).
🌐
Works 100% offline — Everything is saved in your browser. No account needed, no internet required after first load. Your data stays on your device.
👤
Add family members — In Settings > Family Members, add people you cook for. The grocery list and servings scale automatically.
▶ 📅 Planner Tab
➕
Add meals — Click + Add Meal or click any empty slot in the weekly grid. Browse or search 500+ recipes, then click one to assign it.
⚡
Auto-Fill by cuisine NEW — Click the Auto-Fill button to fill your entire week with a cuisine (Italian, Mexican, Asian, etc.). Choose which days to fill, filter by dietary needs, and decide whether to overwrite existing meals.
💰
Weekly budget bar — The red/green bar at the top tracks estimated spending vs. your budget. Edit the budget amount directly in the input field.
📋
Copy Week — Copies the current week's plan to the next week. Great for meal prep routines.
📄
Export PDF — Download your weekly plan as a printable PDF. Three export types: meal plan, grocery list, or full report.
🗑
Remove a meal — Click the × button on any meal card in the planner grid to remove it from that slot.
👶
Kid meal rows NEW — Enable in Settings to add separate kid meal slots (Kid Breakfast, Kid Lunch, etc.) below each day's adult meals. See the Kid Meals section below for details.
▶ 👶 Kid Meals
👶
Enable kid meal rows NEW — Go to Settings > Kid Meals and flip the toggle on. This adds 4 extra meal slots per day: Kid Breakfast, Kid Lunch, Kid Dinner, and Kid Snack — shown below the adult meals with an orange highlight.
🍴
Kid-friendly recipes — Over 650 recipes are auto-tagged as kid-friendly based on ingredients kids typically enjoy (pasta, cheese, chicken, pancakes, etc.) and excluding spicy ingredients. Filter recipes by "kid-friendly" in the Recipes tab.
⚡
Auto-Fill prefers kid recipes — When you use Auto-Fill, kid meal slots automatically prefer kid-friendly tagged recipes so your little ones get meals they'll actually eat.
🛒
Grocery list included — Kid meal ingredients are automatically added to your grocery list and budget tracking — no extra work needed.
🔄
Toggle anytime — Turning kid meals off hides the rows but keeps the data. Turn it back on and your kid meals are still there.
▶ 🍳 Recipes Tab
🔍
Search recipes — Type any keyword (ingredient, dish name, cuisine) in the search bar. Results update instantly.
🍴
Filter by cuisine & diet — Use the dropdown filters to narrow recipes by cuisine type (Italian, Mexican, Asian, etc.) or dietary need (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto, etc.).
➕
Add your own recipes — Click + Add Recipe to create custom recipes with ingredients, prep time, servings, and dietary tags. They appear alongside the built-in 500+ recipes.
📋
Recipe cards — Each card shows cook time, servings, estimated cost, and dietary badges. Click to view full details or assign to a meal slot.
⭐
Favorites — Click the star ☆ on any recipe card to mark it as a favorite. Use the Favorites checkbox above the recipe grid to filter and show only your starred recipes.
👶
Kid-friendly filter NEW — Select "kid-friendly" from the dietary filter to show 650+ recipes tagged as safe and appealing for kids — no spicy ingredients, simple flavours kids love.
▶ 🛒 Grocery Tab
🛒
Auto-generated list — Your grocery list builds automatically from meals you've planned. Duplicate ingredients are combined and organised by category (Dairy, Produce, Protein, etc.).
✅
Check off items — Tick the checkbox next to each item as you shop. Checked items are remembered even if you leave the tab.
❌
Remove individual items NEW — Click the × button next to any item to remove it from the list (e.g. you already have it). Removed items reappear if you regenerate the list.
🧊
Mark What You Have NEW — Click this button to open your inventory and quickly mark items you already own. Matching items show a count so you can skip buying them.
🛒
Shopping links — Click the Amazon button next to items to shop for them online. Links are region-aware based on your currency setting.
🗑
Clear All — Removes all grocery items at once. You'll see a confirmation dialog first to prevent accidental clears.
🧰
Equipment Needed This Week NEW — At the top of the grocery tab, you'll see cooking tools auto-detected from your planned recipes (frying pan, blender, baking sheet, etc.). Click any to shop for it online.
▶ 🧊 Inventory Tab
🧊
Track what you have — Add items you already have at home (pantry staples, leftovers, etc.). This helps the grocery list know what you don't need to buy.
🔄
Syncs with grocery list — When you view the grocery tab, items matching your inventory are highlighted. Use the "Mark What You Have" button on the grocery tab for a quick side-by-side check.
🗑
Clear All Inventory — Start fresh after a big shop. Confirmation dialog prevents accidents.
▶ 📊 Dashboard Tab
📊
Weekly overview — See charts for your spending vs. budget, calorie breakdown, and nutrition balance for the planned week.
💰
Budget tracking — The dashboard shows how your estimated grocery spend compares to your weekly budget. Adjust your budget on the Planner tab.
🍴
Meal coverage — Quickly see how many meal slots are filled vs. empty across the week.
▶ 💚 Health Tab
💚
Completely optional — The health profile helps suggest better recipes but you can skip every field. Nothing is required.
📏
Auto-detects your units NEW — Height/weight labels switch automatically based on your currency region. US/CA/MX show inches & lbs, others show cm & kg. Change anytime in Settings.
⚠
Medical disclaimer — This app is a planning tool, not medical advice. After saving your profile, you'll see a disclaimer and then be guided back to the Planner to start cooking.
🏥
Find a dietitian — Links to registered dietitian directories are provided based on your region for professional guidance.
▶ ⚙ Settings Tab
✍
Planner name — Change the title in the header anytime. Try your family name, a fun phrase, or keep the default.
💰
Currency & region — Supports 11 currencies (CAD, USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, and more). Changing this updates budget display, shopping links, and health units.
🌑
Dark mode — Toggle dark/light theme. Also available via the moon/sun button in the header.
👀
Accessibility — High contrast mode, four color-blind presets (deuteranopia, protanopia, tritanopia, monochromacy), and adjustable font size.
🔔
Reminders — Set planning and shopping reminders with custom day and time. Uses browser notifications (with in-app fallback).
💾
Backup & restore — Export your data as JSON backup or CSV. Import to restore on a new device. Clear All Data resets everything (with confirmation).
👶
Kid Meals NEW — Toggle on separate kid meal rows for each day in the planner. Kid-friendly recipes are auto-suggested when using Auto-Fill.
▶ 💡 Tips & Tricks
⚡
Fastest way to plan a week — Use Auto-Fill to populate the whole week by cuisine, then swap out individual meals you don't like.
💰
Stay under budget — Watch the budget bar on the Planner tab. Swap expensive meals for cheaper ones until the bar turns green.
📦
Meal prep Sunday — Plan your week, export the grocery PDF on Saturday, shop on Sunday, and use the inventory tab to track what you bought.
🔄
Copy & tweak — Found a week you love? Use Copy Week to duplicate it, then make small changes the following week for variety.
👤
Cooking for one? — Look for recipes marked "1 srv" or "2 srv" to avoid waste and keep costs down.
💾
Back up regularly — Go to Settings > Data Management > Backup (JSON) every so often. If you clear your browser data, your plan will be lost without a backup.
📱
Works on mobile — On smaller screens, use the ☰ hamburger menu in the top-right corner to switch between tabs. The entire app is fully responsive.
👶
Planning for the family? — Add family members in Settings, then enable Kid Meals to give your children their own meal slots. Auto-Fill picks kid-friendly recipes automatically.
